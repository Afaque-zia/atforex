5. What is Forex Leverage?

Leverage involves borrowing a certain amount of money needed to invest in something. In the case of forex, money is usually borrowed from a broker. Forex trading does offer high leverage in the sense that for an initial margin requirement, a trader can build up—and control—a huge amount of money. for example If a Forex Trader has an account leverage of 100:1and they wish to use $1,000 on one Forex trade as margin, they will have exposure of $100,000 in base currency ($1,000) = 100 x $1,000 = $100,000 (trade value). and there are different laverage services by different brokers, At Forex Academy will not be responsible for your leverage.
